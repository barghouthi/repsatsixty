Reps at Sixty Workshop
Co-located with SAS 2016
Edinburgh, UK
September 11 2016

We will be holding a one-day event celebrating Tom Reps’ sixtieth birthday, in honor of his numerous contributions to the field of Programming Languages, especially in the discipline of Static Program Analysis. It will be held on September 11th at Edinburgh, UK, co-located with the 23rd Static Analysis Symposium (SAS). The event will consist of a collection of invited talks. Currently confirmed speakers include Patrick Cousot, Neil Immerman, Tim Teitelbaum, and Tom Ball, All are welcome. Please contact the organizers for more information or suggestions.
